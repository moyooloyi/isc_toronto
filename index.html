Welcome to (ISC)2 Toronto Chapter

Become a Member

It is a pleasure to have you here!

We are the Toronto Chapter of (ISC)2 a non-profit association primarily for information security officials in Toronto. We aim to build a network of peers to share knowledge, exchange resources and collaborate on projects within the local community. However, The (ISC)2 Toronto Chapter welcomes members of (ISC)2, non-(ISC)2 credential holders, and information technology and security practictioners to join us

Our Purpose

We exist to provide educational opportunities for management, operational and technical aspects of the information technology field, and to support the vision of ISC(2), which is to inspire a safe and secure cyber world while nurturing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals. Beyond these, we are a community of like-minded, gracious individuals. Read more...

Who can join the (ISC)2 Toronto Chapter?

Existing or intending information security professionals

Residents of Toronto, Canada

(ISC)2 certified information security professionals
